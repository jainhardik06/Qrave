## ‚úÖ GLOSSARY OF TERMS

### Stock Management Terms
- **SKU** (Stock Keeping Unit): Unique identifier for item
- **Reorder Level**: Minimum stock before ordering
- **Lead Time**: Days to receive after ordering
- **FIFO**: First In First Out (use old items first)
- **Safety Stock**: Buffer to prevent stock-outs
- **Variance**: Difference between expected & actual stock

### Procurement Terms
- **PO** (Purchase Order): Request to buy from supplier
- **GRN** (Goods Receipt Note): Delivery verification
- **Payment Terms**: When/how to pay (COD, Credit days)
- **Quality Check**: Verify delivered items are good
- **Discrepancy**: Mismatch between ordered & received

### Operations Terms
- **Batch Number**: Identifier for items from same shipment
- **Expiry Date**: When item becomes unusable
- **Cycle Counting**: Regular physical count of high-value items
- **Shrinkage**: Missing inventory (theft/spillage/error)
- **Yield**: Cooked weight as % of raw weight

### Analytics Terms
- **COGS** (Cost of Goods Sold): Total ingredient cost per dish
- **Margin**: Profit $ or % on item
- **Turnover**: How fast inventory sells
- **DIO** (Days Inventory Outstanding): Days to sell inventory
- **Forecast**: Predicted future demand

### AI/Automation Terms
- **Demand Forecasting**: Predict how much you'll need
- **Auto-Reordering**: System creates PO automatically
- **Smart Recommendations**: AI suggests actions
- **Optimization**: Finding best approach (supplier, quantity, timing)
